[Pages 15-22: Analysis of Channel Stuffing Practices]
Meridian engaged in systematic channel stuffing by recording revenue on
transactions that had not been finalized. Key instances include:
- Apex Technologies: $3.2M in premature revenue recognition
- Bridgewater Systems: $2.8M in accelerated bookings
- Cascade Networks: $2.3M in bill-and-hold arrangements

[Pages 23-27: Revenue Overstatement Analysis]
Total Revenue Overstatement: $8,300,000
Reported Revenue:  $52,800,000
Actual Revenue:    $44,500,000

Corrected EBITDA:  $12,100,000 (vs. reported $18,400,000)
